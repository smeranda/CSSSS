Social Media "Strategy"

  • We don't plug into social media.
  • Social media becomes part of our communications strategy.

Objectives

  • Objectives define tactics
  • We already have these!

For the immediate timeframe, it will be valuable to show your social media marketing is effective at your overarching goals. Therefore, create goals and related metrics that track your progress and allow you to take actions based on insights to help keep the train on the track.

Tactics

  • How we implement
  • Set targets (milestones)

Setting targets (3,000 pageviews instead of 2,500) forces the organization to shift from maintenance mode to problem-solving, business-building mode. Thinking through challenges and finding new ways to make things work better. Setting targets also creates accountability.

Our Admissions Example

  1. Objective: Increase out-of-state students
  2. Target: 1,100 out-of-state incoming student enrollments for 2011-2012 cycle

Traditional Admissions Funnel

Primary KPIs

  • Positive online mentions of UNL leading to 2011
  • Net new "Likes" of UNL content from out-of-state users
    • Facebook likes/fans
    • Twitter favorites/followers
    • 16-18 year olds
  • Net new click-throughs of links leading to admissions.unl.edu from out-of-state students
    • Tie these to goals!

Secondary KPIs

  • Net new "likes" of UNL content from in-state 16-18 year olds
  • Net new click-throughs of links leading to admissions.unl.edu from in-state students
